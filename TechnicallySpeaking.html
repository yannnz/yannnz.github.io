Assistive Tech /Internet of Things

A communication assistant & room facility control App for ICU patient

ABOUT

TechnicallySpeaking is an opensource project develped for NYU Langone Hospital ICU patients. The goal of this project is to create a solution that is user friendly for patient in the ICU, family and staff so that the patient can make their physical, psychological, and environmental needs known, improve comfort, and increase independence in both simple and critical medical decisions.














My Role



Product Design UX Design UI Design

Our team members are all from different backgrounds: hardware engineer, ICU medical assistant, developer. I play design role in this project, and I was able to work those amazing team members starting from research to the final delivery of our project.
